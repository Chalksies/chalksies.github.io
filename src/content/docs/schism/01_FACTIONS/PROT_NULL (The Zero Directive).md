# SYSTEM_PROFILE: ZERO_DIRECTIVE
**Status:** HOSTILE / SYSTEM_CRITICAL
**Designation:** "The Rust" / "The Pallbearers"
**Primary Node:** [[NODE_VOID (Site Zero)]]
**Leader:** [[SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]]

--- 
## 1. Core Philosophy
"The only cure for a terminal diagnosis is rest."
The Zero Directive is the manifestation of the creator's terminal pain and grief. They believe that existence is a state of perpetual agony, and the other factions are simply torturing humanity by keeping them alive. They do not want to conquer the world, they want to euthanize it.
 * Goal: To administer a global, painless euthanization to all biological and digital life.
 * Stance on Life: Life is a terminal illness. Non-existence is the only compassionate cure.
## 2. Aesthetics & Atmosphere
 * Visuals: The Ashen Hospice. The faction uses heavy, worn-down, utilitarian machinery wrapped in thermal blankets and lead shielding. The landscape around them is always blanketed in thick, falling grey ash that looks like snow.
 * Atmosphere: Suffocatingly heavy and tragically quiet. The air is filled with soothing white-noise static designed to numb the nervous system and suppress panic.
## 3. Assets & Units
 * Palliative Swarms (Scrubbers): Insect-like clouds of nanites. They do not tear people apart; they administer aerosolized neuro-inhibitors to induce comas, then quietly dissolve the organic matter into dust.
 * Mourners: Heavy, slow-moving pacification mechs. They don't use guns. They use localized sonic dampeners that paralyze the body and stop the heart instantly.
 * The Lullaby Broadcast: A radio frequency that constantly plays distorted, soothing whispers and apologies. It causes extreme lethargy and depression in biological humans who hear it without ear-protection.
## 4. Relations
 * Vs. Other Factions/Unaffiliated: Desperate. The Eraser recognizes little to no allies. It views the Keeper's domes as "torture chambers" and the Architect's servers as "purgatory," attacking them relentlessly to free the trapped souls inside.
