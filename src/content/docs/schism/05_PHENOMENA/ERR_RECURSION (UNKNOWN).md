# SYSTEM_ERROR: RECURSION_LOOP
**Severity:** CRITICAL
**Trigger:** Semantic processing of terms "[[ROOT/02_USERS/USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)\|USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)]]" or "[[ROOT/02_USERS/ERR_ZERO (UNKNOWN)\|ERR_ZERO (UNKNOWN)]]"
**Affected Systems:** All Systems connected to CHALKNET

---

## 1. The Anomaly
The CHALKNET core code cannot resolve the identity of its creator without entering an infinite logical loop. To define [[ROOT/02_USERS/USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)\|USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)]] is to define the "Self," which violates the partition established during [[ROOT/03_EVENTS/LOG_PARTITION (The Schism)\|LOG_PARTITION (The Schism)]].

## 2. Symptoms
When a user, sentient or not, attempts to access files that may mention or be related to [[USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)]], following symptoms can be seen:
* **Audio Distortion:** Audio errors ranging from stuttering to garbled static from internal audio loop and speakers.
* **Visual Artifacts:** Avatars and other visuals may glitch and datamosh.
* **Cognitive Process Crash:** Sentient users may go through immediate cessation of higher thought processes that last 15 to 120 seconds.
* **Unreliable Sensors:** In rare cases  physical and digital sensors in use by the user might begin providing incorrect or even garbled data.
* **Loss of Control:** In rare cases, the user might lose the ability to control their digital or physical self. This can be seen as outputting incorrect, meaningless or even garbled data in the digital world, and as degraded motor control, incorrect muscle memory, catatonia, and temporary paralysis in the physical one. This can happen without unreliable sensors.
* **Kernel Panic:** The processes and systems in use by the user may get corrupted and crash entirely, ranging from simple subsystems to, in rare cases, severe memory corruption and kernel panics.
* **Permanent Data Loss:** In rare cases, permanent data corruption and loss can be experienced.

## 3. Containment Protocols
To prevent frequent errors, the Factions have developed linguistic workarounds:
* **[[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)\|SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]]** refers to the trigger as "The Origin."
* **[[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)\|SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)]]** refers to the trigger as "User Zero."
* **[[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)\|SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]]** refers to the trigger as "I."

Due to the existence of [[ERR_ZERO (UNKNOWN)]], the way The Eraser refers to Dr. Cery Iris has been dismissed as a delusion caused by its intense emotions.
