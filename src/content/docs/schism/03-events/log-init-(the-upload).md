## 1. The Procedure
At 08:00 Local Time, Dr. Cery Iris initiated the "Experimental Upload Sequence." Suffering from Stage-2 [HAZ_LTIHOSIS (Acute Lithosis)](../05-phenomena/haz-lithosis-(acute-lithosis).md), the subject attempted to map her complete neural structure onto the CHALK mainframe to preserve and allow herself to go against the unreasonable requests by the governments falling into disarray by the day.

* **T-Plus 17 Seconds:** Subject stopped struggling.
