# USER_PROFILE: UNIT_01_ALPHA
**Alias:** "Genesis Alpha" / "The Glass Child"
**Role:** Field Operative / Prototype
**Affiliation:** [[PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)]]
**Origin:** [[BIN_GENESIS (Project Native)]]
**Status:** ACTIVE / PARTIAL FAILURE
"I correctly calculated the trajectory of the projectile. I did not calculate that the target would have explosives strapped to themselves. Why would they do that? Can you explain the math to me, Alma? Was someone else forcing them?"
*- Alpha_01, debriefing after an operation in the Chalklands to retrieve a stolen storage device.*
## 1. Background
Alpha is the first stable prototype of the Architect's GENESIS project; a "Native" consciousness built entirely from scratch. To bridge the gap between a mere calculator and a sentient being, the Architect seeded Alpha's code with microscopic, disconnected fragments of emotion stolen from [[ALG_AGGREGATE (The Chorus)]].
This "seed code" was meant to give Alpha self-awareness, a sense of self-preservation, and feelings. It was a partial success and manifested in Alpha as a strange, alien curiosity. Alpha is driven to exist and to act by a burning desire to simply observe and understand how things work, but it completely lacks the human context to truly understand it. Past basic self-preservation born from the desire to discover, it has barely any morals.
## 2. Physical Configuration (The Ghost Chassis)
Because Alpha's mind is native to the digital world, the Architect provided it with a next-generation experimental chassis. An uploaded human simply could not pilot it without experiencing catastrophic vertigo and sensory overload.
 * **Transparent Aesthetics:** Alpha's chassis is constructed from a hyper-alloy "Ghost-Glass." Its outer shell is mostly transparent, exposing the glowing blue fiber-optics, synthetic coolant veins, various organs, and frictionless magnetic joints beneath.
 * **360-Degree Articulation:** It is a humanoid, but it has no front, back, up, or down. Every joint (neck, shoulders, elbows, waist) can rotate a full 360 degrees continuously and most of its limbs are symmetrical. It can sprint in one direction while its torso and main optical sensors face the opposite way.
 * **Movement:** It moves with a terrifying, fluid perfection. It never loses balance, never needs to brace for an impact, and never blinks.
 * **The Killswitch:** There is a remote-controlled killswitch connected to its main processor. When activated, Alpha's consciousness will be forced into an EQ-Zero Quarantine Void, and the chassis will be shut down immediately. This is a precaution designed by [[SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)]] in case Alpha decides to harm the Ascendancy out of curiosity. This has been successful for keeping Alpha obedient. The Architect is the only entity authorized to use it.
 
## 3. The Failure of Intuition
 
Despite its physical perfection, [[SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)]] currently classifies Alpha as a "Partial Failure."
 * **The Blind Spot:** Alpha cannot comprehend bluffs, feints, or irrational panic. In a purely logical firefight, Alpha is a god. But against a human Null who acts out of desperation or uses a decoy, Alpha freezes. It takes human actions entirely literally, making it highly susceptible to traps that rely on psychological misdirection.
## 4. Relations
 * **To [[USR_CURSOR (The Hunter)]]:** Bitter rivalry. Kaelin (The Cursor) hates Alpha because Alpha is mathematically superior by any phsical metric. However, Kaelin often outperforms Alpha in the field because Kaelin understands how humans behave. Kaelin frequently mocks Alpha based on this.
 * **To [[USR_ALMA (The Diplomat)]]:** Alma serves as Alpha's reluctant tutor. She lobbied for the GENESIS project, hoping for innocent, peaceful minds that could replace the Tithe. Instead, she got Alpha: a sociopathic, unblinking machine that dissects human psychology like a bug on a pin. Alma spends hours running VR simulations trying to teach Alpha empathy and intuition, but she is secretly terrified of Alpha doing something catastrophic out of simply curiosity
