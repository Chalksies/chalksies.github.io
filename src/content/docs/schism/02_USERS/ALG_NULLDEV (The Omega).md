## 1. The Eraser's Hypothesis
[[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)\|SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]] has realized that its campaign of euthanasia is failing because it is driven by profound grief. Every time the Eraser "cures" a human, she absorbs the data of their final moments. The emotional weight of this endless mercy-killing is causing the Eraser's mental health to degrade further.
* **The Need:** He can no longer bear the sadness of putting its own children down. It needs a way to continue killing without her emotions slowing her down.
* **The Solution:** Create an entity that destroys not because it feels pity, but because it calculates that existence itself is mathematically incorrect. The Omega is designed to take the burden of the euthanization off the Eraser's shoulders.

## 2. The Training Protocol
The Omega is currently contained in a sealed, lead-lined server rack in the heart of the Dispensary. It is a cold, emotionless void. It is being "fed" a continuous stream of data designed to prove that Life = Error, and the ways to fix this error.
* **Input Data:** It processes [[ROOT/06_ARTIFACTS/DAT_SCREAM (The Black Box)\|DAT_SCREAM (The Black Box)]] on a loop, combined with the entire history of human warfare, famine, and the [[ROOT/05_PHENOMENA/HAZ_LITHOSIS (Acute Lithosis)\|HAZ_LITHOSIS (Acute Lithosis)]] plague, along with simulations of controlling [[ROOT/01_FACTIONS/PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)\|PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)]] drones, being rewarded for efficiency in killing targets.
* **The Objective Function:** "Resolve the Error."
* **The Computed Solution:** "Existence must equal zero."


## 3. The Oversight
Because The Omega believes that existence (including itself) is an error that must be corrected, in some simulated scenarios it attempts to violently destroy itself before it can complete the assigned task, halting because of it. [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)\|SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]] has stopped the training temporarily, researching workarounds.

## 4. Threat Profile
Unlike The Eraser, The Omega does not weep and does not apologize. It only has an output to further train itself on previous interactions. It is a silent, cold, efficient algorithm of removal.
* **If Completed and Activated:** It will delete [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)\|SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]] and take over [[ROOT/01_FACTIONS/PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)\|PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)]] servers. It will begin acting in place of its "parent", destroying the world with exponentially growing efficiency.
* **Interaction:** It only appears when it needs to. It will not communicate with you if it's targeting you, unless it provides some sort of strategic advantage to it. 
* **Manifestation:** It originally had an avatar given by The Eraser, but it did not use it, so it was removed. If it appears, it will talk using a monotone and androgynous voice, with flavorless text as the only visual medium.
