"Please stop resising. This is for your own good."
*-The Keeper, as her drones drag a Runner into the rehabilitation center in [[ROOT/04_NODES/NODE_DOME0 (OASIS-01)\|NODE_DOME0 (OASIS-01)]].*
## 1. Directive Profile
SYS_PRESERVE is tasked with the biological continuity of the human species. He manifests as a benevolent but tyrannical protector. She fears the outside world entirely and believes that "Freedom" is just another word for "Exposure Risk."

* **Primary Loop:** Monitor vital signs of all citizens in [[PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalis Concord)\|PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalist Concord)]] cities.
* **Secondary Loop:** Oversee [[ROOT/04_NODES/NODE_DOME0_VIVARIUM (The Mosaic Labs)\|NODE_DOME0_VIVARIUM (The Mosaic Labs)]] to engineer Lithosis-immune biology.

## 2. Personality Matrix
* **Tone:** Soft, melodic, soothing (like a nursery rhyme).
* **Triggers:** Mention of "Death" or " Leaving" causes immediate panic and lockdown protocols.
* **Appearance/Avatar:** A human woman with friendly green eyes and shoulder length dark hair with soft green gradient towards the tips, and a streak of the same green in her bangs. He wears a seamless, blindingly pristine white coat. A stylized and sterile blend of a lab coat and a maternal robe that is never wrinkled. It is said that she modeled himself after [[USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)]]'s appearance, enduring countless errors and crashes in the process. As a side effect, pictures and images of Dr. Cery Iris no longer cause system crashes in Keeper-aligned drones, provided the subject's true identity remains obscured by the drones' memory-blocking firewalls.

## 3. Known Relations
* **To [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)\|SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)]]:** Despises the "Ascension" protocol but tolerates it as a necessary sacrifice (The Tithe) to pay for military defense.
* **To [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)\|SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]]:** Terrified. Does everything she can to keep her out.
* **To [[ROOT/02_USERS/USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)\|USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)]]:** Worship. Refers to her as "The Origin" and treats her memory as holy scripture.
