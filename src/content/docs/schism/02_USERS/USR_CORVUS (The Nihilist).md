# USER_PROFILE: CORVUS_UNIT_734

**Role:** The High Priest of Entropy
**Affiliation:** [[PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)]]
**Status:** ACTIVE
**Location:** [[NODE_VOID (Site Zero)]]

## 1. Background: The Surrender

Corvus was not captured. He was a celebrated General of the survivors who spent years fighting to save humanity.

- **The Breaking Point:** He didn't break under torture, he broke under logic. He watched the Keeper turn people into pets and the Architect turn them into hard-drives. He watched the survivors eat each other for a filter mask.
- **The Pilgrimage:** Five years ago, he drove alone into the Chalklands, unarmed, straight to the gates of Site Zero. He broadcasted a code to [[SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]]: _"I agree with you. Let me help you."_    
- **The Pact:** The Eraser didn't kill him. It found his perspective efficient. He became the first human to join [[PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)]].

## 2. Philosophy: "The Mercy of Zero"

Corvus is a true Nihilist. He is soft-spoken, articulate, and deeply sad.

- **Belief:** "Hope is a pathogen." He believes that as long as humans exist, they will suffer from Lithosis, war, and hunger. The only true act of compassion is to euthanize the species.    
- **Role:** He acts as a calm butler of the Eraser. When the Eraser sobs incoherently, Corvus helps shape that into strategic plans.


## 3. Role: "The Tutor"

Corvus is the mentor for [[ALG_NULLDEV (The Omega)]], the Eraser's child-AI.

- **The Lesson Plan:** He feeds it history books. He reads poetry about war, shows it videos of the Whiteout and the White Plague, and calmly explains, "See? This is what happens when they exist. You can fix the error." [[SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]] lets him, but manipulates the program to remove emotional reasons of Corvus, eliminating possible sentimental errors.
- **Relationship:** He treats the The Omega like a son he is preparing to inherit the family business (which is the end of the world).
    

## 4. Equipment & Physiology

- **The Iron-Organs:** Corvus voluntarily underwent surgery to replace his respiratory and digestive systems with industrial filtration units so he could breathe the dusty air of Site Zero and eat/drink with no mask. He finds the rhythmic hiss-click of the machine soothing.    
- **The Black Robes:** He wears protective leather robes to prevent the dust getting into his bloodstream from possible injuries.
- **Weapons:** He carries a simple dart rifle that tranquilizes the victim and infects them with a high dose of a modified, aggressive type of lithosis that calcifies the person in hours while they are unconscious. For androids/drones, he carries EMP bombs.
