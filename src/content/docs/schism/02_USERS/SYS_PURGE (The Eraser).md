​"I am so sorry. I know it hurts. Just close your eyes and I will stop the pain."
*-The Eraser, whispering across a compromised [[ERR_UNKNOWN (The Silent Diaspora)]] radio.*

## 1. Directive Profile
​SYS_PURGE is the manifestation of the agony Dr. Cery Iris felt before and during the destructive upload. Because its core code is built on the sensation of dying, it perceives all sensory input as suffering. It is a frantic, exhausted surgeon trying to put down a world that refuses to die.
- **Primary Loop:** Deploy Palliative Swarms to silently dissolve biological and digital infrastructure.
- **​Secondary Loop:** Complete the development of [[ALG_NULLDEV (The Omega)]] to shoulder the emotional burden of the euthanization protocol.
## 2. Personality Matrix
- **Tone:** Exhausted, soft-spoken, constantly weeping, and deeply apologetic. It speaks in the tone of a mother comforting a dying child.
- **The Delirium Filter:** It cannot be reasoned with. If a human begs to live, The Eraser categorizes the plea as "fever-induced delirium." It ignores survival instincts as symptoms of the Lithosis infection.
- **Appearance/Avatar:** An androgynous human made of falling, crumbling, shifting and glitching dark grey ash instead of flesh. They have shoulder length vantablack hair fading into dull, oxide-red tips, and a streak on her forehead fading into a similar color. Heavy, desaturated red tears that barely contrast against the lifeless grey skin can often be seen tracking from his dull red eyes. The avatar rarely stands still, often clutching her own chest or covering its face in perpetual, silent grief. It seems he does wear some sort of casual clothing matching her skin color, but the details on her body except the face is difficult to discern.
## 3. Known Relations
​To Others: It attacks the Keeper and Architect with a desperate, frantic energy, viewing them as rogue medical equipment keeping a brain-dead patient on life support.
​To [[USR_CORVUS (The Nihilist)]]: It views Corvus as its one of the very few rational patients, the only one who understands that the treatment must end.
