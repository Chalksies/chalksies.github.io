# USER_PROFILE: ELARA_07
**Role:** High Liaison / Public Relations Officer
**Affiliation:** [[PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalis Concord)\|PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalist Concord)]]
**Safety Score:** 98.8 (Platinum)

"The Silver Train is for those who gave up. The White Train is for those who fought back. And I have to stand on the platform and smile for both."
_- Elara, in her personal diary_

## 1. Background
Elara is the "Face" of [[ROOT/04_NODES/NODE_DOME0 (OASIS-01)\|NODE_DOME0 (OASIS-01)]]. When the Keeper needs to speak to the populace without using a terrifying hologram, she uses Elara. She oversees the Tithe for the Keeper, regularly interacting and negotiating with [[USR_ALMA (The Diplomat)]] despite them not changing anything. She is broadcasted on screens across the Dome, speaking in press conferences, educational programs, and most notably, announcing the monthly "Paradise Lottery."

## 2. The Diplomatic Cold War
Elara serves as the primary point of contact for [[USR_ALMA (The Diplomat)]], the Architect's synthetic diplomat.
- **The Public Act:** Together, Elara and Alma film propaganda promoting the "Silver Line" to the extent [[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]] allows, painting a picture of a beautiful off-world colony where sickness doesn't exist.
- **The Private Reality:** Elara respects Alma's goals, but still disagrees. While Alma genuinely believes uploading is a mercy, Elara thinks it is just digital suicide. They meet weekly for formal negotiations, sipping tea while quietly battling over human lives with little to no changes to the status quo.


## 3. Personality & The Secret 
Elara is a trapped celebrity.
- **The Guilt:** Publicly, she is cheerful, patriotic, and perfectly obedient. Privately, she is being crushed under the guilt of the [[BIN_TITHE (The Preservation Protocol)]].
- **The Resistance:** She is secretly a high-ranking member of the "Runners" (the internal Dome resistance). She is horrified by the "White Line" because the Keeper's algorithm specifically selects low Safety Score citizens to meet the quota. This means the Keeper is systematically using the Tithe to remove political dissidents, many free-thinkers, and even Elara's fellow Runners.
# 4. Equipment & Espionage
To survive her role, Elara utilizes smuggled tech.
- **The Neural-Dampener:** She wears a concealed, EMP-shielded earring crafted from scavenged [[PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)]] tech by some Nulls. This device spoofs the Keeper’s biometric emotional monitoring, projecting "calm and compliant" baseline. It allows her to lie to the Keeper, smile at Alma, and announce executions without her heart rate triggering a Safety Score drop. Since she was always obedient and earned her score normally, the Keeper doesn't see anything out of the ordinary.
- **The Smuggler:** She uses her Platinum-tier security clearance to bypass Nanny Drone checkpoints, smuggling un-censored history books, artworks, old world tech and various forbidden objects into the residential blocks to keep the human spirit alive.
