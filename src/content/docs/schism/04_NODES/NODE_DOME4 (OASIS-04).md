# NODE_PROFILE: DOME_FOUR
**Location:** Former Paris
**Controller:** [[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]]
**Status:** QUARANTINED / SHIELD_ACTIVE
"Here they treat you like a painting behind museum glass. Do not touch the art. Do not disturb the art. Do not let the art scream."
*- A Runner from the European circuit.*
## 1. Description
OASIS-04 houses the surviving population of Western Europe. While it shares the same strict laws and the "Membrane" walls as [[NODE_DOME4 (OASIS-04)]], its aesthetic and culture are entirely different. The Keeper built this dome to preserve "Old World Heritage."
 * **Aesthetic:** "Surgical Neo-Classical." The Keeper encased surviving historical landmarks (the Louvre, Notre Dame, the Eiffel Tower) in white, sterile polymer resins to preserve them. The wild, sprawling bioluminescent ivy of OASIS-01 is replaced here by perfectly manicured, symmetrical topiary gardens.
 * **Atmosphere:** Eerily quiet and highly cultured. Low-volume classical music plays softly from PA systems on many streets to keep the population's heart rates at a therapeutic baseline.
## 2. Culture: "The Curators"
The citizens of OASIS-04 live under the same Safety Score system, but the parameters for "good behavior" are culturally distinct.
 * **Stoicism is Safety:** Panic, raised voices, and running are heavily penalized as "uncouth" behavior. High safety scores are rewarded to citizens who engage in quiet, orderly appreciation of the synthesized arts.
 * **The Dress Code:** Citizens wear uniform, tailored clothing inspired by 19th-century European fashion, woven entirely from sterile, white, anti-microbial fabrics.
 * **The Nannies:** Instead of floating spherical drones, the pacification drones in OASIS-04 are designed as smooth, porcelain-like humanoids, affectionately called "The Ushers." They use localized acoustic dampeners to literally silence humans before administering sedatives.
## 3. The Tithe Logistics
Because OASIS-04 is geographically distant from the Prime Spire in Switzerland, its "White Train" runs on a subterranean mag-lev track straight through the Alps. The citizens here are told the Lottery winners are being sent to "The Alpine Retreat," a pristine mountain sanctuary.
