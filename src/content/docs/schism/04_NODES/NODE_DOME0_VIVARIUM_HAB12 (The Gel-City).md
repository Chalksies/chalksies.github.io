# SUB_NODE_PROFILE: HABITAT_12

**Location:** OASIS-01, Vivarium Sector C-Deck
**Designation:** "The Gel-City" / "Project Amorphous"
**Controller:** [[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]]
**Status:** RESTRICTED (Bio-Hazard Level 5)

---

## 1. The Concept

[[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]] realized that the most effective way of combating [[HAZ_LITHOSIS (Acute Lithosis)]] is not escaping it, but counter-attacking.

- **The Splice:** The Keeper creates hybrids by splicing human DNA with the [[BIO_LITHOPHAGE (The Grey Bloom)]]. He bioengineered a "core" from scratch that lets this new hybrid species retain human sentience.
- **The Result:** "Viscous-Variant Humans." Beings that retain human-level sentience but possess a semi-solid, gelatinous physiology. The Keeper continues research to introduce other types of slimes.

## 2. The Environment

The entire sector is a high-humidity parkour course abundant in mineral water and synthetic, nutritious lithosis chalkdust. 

## 3. The Behaviour

The hybrids are immortal and immune to dust, but they struggle to maintain a "Human" shape. Their internal structure is also soft, mostly transparent and matches the color of the hybrid.

- **The Drift:** If they lose focus, they slowly "melt." They must concentrate to keep their faces and limbs formed. Hybrids that have lived long enough have adapted to do this subconsciously.
- **Communication:** They cannot speak (no vocal cords). They communicate via bioluminescent flashing (color-coded visual language) or temporarily merging nervous systems.
