# NODE_PROFILE: DOME_ZERO
**Location:** Former Singapore Archipelago
**Controller:** [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)\|SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]]
**Status:** QUARANTINED / SHIELD_ACTIVE

---

## 1. Description
The larges4 bastion of biological humanity and the capital of [[PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalis Concord)]]. OASIS-01 is a vertical garden city encased in a hexagonal hard-light energy shield. It is a utopia designed to be a prison; safe, clean, and surveilled.
* **Aesthetic:** "Sterile Solarpunk." White curves, genetically modified ivy sprawled over residences, and bioluminescent night-lighting.
* **Population:** ~11 Million (Projected stable).

## 2. Key Sectors
* **The Nursery:** Residential blocks where citizens live in "Smart Pods" that monitor their hormone levels. Even at night, the pods and streets are monitored by cameras and drones, with a soft glow partially lighting the streets.
* **The Preservation Vaults:** Museums containing the DNA of extinct animals preserved by [[USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)]] and a lot of censored human art.
* **The Tithe Gates:** The heavily guarded spaceport where the Architect collects its "Human Quota."
* **The Membrane:** The giant walls around the dome which the shield is equipped on top of. It also houses maintencance tunnels for the air intakes and exhaust.

## 3. Security
* **Nanny Drones:** Non-lethal enforcement units equipped with tasers and sedatives.
* **Social Credit:** Access to food types and luxury is determined by a citizen's "Safety Score."
