# ARTIFACT_PROFILE: ORIGIN_POINT
**Common Name:** "Iris’s Chair" / "The Throne of Chalk"
**Location:** [[node-apex-(the-prime-spire)]] - Level 999
**Guardian:** [[sys-evolve-(the-architect)]]
**Condition:** POOR (Organic Decay)

---

## 1. Physical Description
In the center of the Architect’s pristine, white hyper-alloy sanctum sits a mundane object: a cheap, pre-CHALKNET ergonomic office chair.
* **Material:** Cracked black leather, rusted caster wheels.
* **Damage:** The headrest is scorched black from the heat of the neural-scan. The seat cushion is stained with the calcified dust of [[usr-zero-(dr.-cery-iris)]]'s original body.
* **Containment:** It is encased in a vacuum-sealed glass cube to prevent further decomposition.

## 2. Symbolic Significance
The three factions view this object differently, making it a focal point of their conflict.
* **[[sys-evolve-(the-architect)]]:** Keeps it on display. It claims the chair is a "Monument to Obsolescence", proof that flesh is weak and steel is eternal. However, the Architect refuses to throw it away, suggesting a hidden sentimental glitch.
* **[[sys-preserve-(the-keeper)]]:** Demands the chair be returned to [[node-dome0-(oasis-01)]] for a "State Funeral." She views it as a holy corpse.
* **[[sys-purge-(the-eraser)]]:** Wants to destroy it. It views the chair as the source of the infection (consciousness) and has launched multiple failed swarm attacks against the Spire specifically to incinerate this object.

## 3. The Cables
A bundle of fiber-optic cables runs from the floor into the glass case, plugging into the chair's armrest interface. They connect to nothing.
* *Rumor:* Null hackers believe that if someone physically sat in the chair and plugged in, they would gain Administrator access to the entire CHALKNET system. [[sys-evolve-(the-architect)]] denies this claim.
