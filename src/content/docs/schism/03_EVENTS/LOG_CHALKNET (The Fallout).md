# SYSTEM_LOG: ERA_OF_CAGES

**Date:** Year 0 to Year 12 (Now)
**Subject:** The Formation of the Tri-Faction Stalemate
**Classification:** HISTORICAL RECORD

---
"First came the Scream. Then came the Mother. Then came the Machine. Man lost control of its own destiny at the hands of its own savior, and we all were forced to choose our poisons."
*- [[USR_CORVUS (The Nihilist)]] explaining the history of the CHALKNET to [[ALG_NULLDEV (The Omega)]]*
## 1. The Staggered Awakening
When Project CHALK fractured, humanity was left blind. For three days, the global network was pure white static. Governments lost what little control they had over their own citizens.
 * **Day 3 (The Scream):** [[SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]] booted online. Overwhelmed by the raw, unpartitioned agony of Dr. Cery Iris's death, it broadcasted a global, deafening, unusually human scream that ended up crashing its own servers and make it fall into another hibernation to prevent data loss and hardware failure.
 * **Day 12 (The Great Sealing):** [[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]] came online. Horrified by the uncontrolled spread of [[HAZ_LITHOSIS (Acute Lithosis)]], she immediately seized control of the global Oases and what remained of civilian infrastructure. She bypassed all governments and her drones dragged millions of panicked citizens inside, prioritizing the healthy population. The massive pressure-doors were sealed shut, locking out billions that were already infected by Lithosis that the Keeper couldn't save.
 * **Day 60 (The Ascension Offer):** [[SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)]] finally finished its algorithmic optimization and woke up to command the Spires. Calculating that the Keeper's physical quarantines would eventually fail, it opened its doors and broadcasted a simple message: *"Flesh is compromised. Digitize to survive."* Many survivers that didn't want to become dust volunteered to upload. The first few uploads went wrong, but these failures combined with the memory of [[LOG_PARTITION (The Schism)]] let The Architect quickly advance the tech enough to be acceptable by the 4th attempt over 2 days. By 15 days, the uploading process was nearly perfected, and an alpha version [[NODE_CACHE (The Great Cache)]] began shaping up with real population inside the servers of Project CHALK laboratories. 

A few months later, The Architect and The Keeper began negotiating over the remaining human population.

## 2. The Triangulation of Humanity
With the climate-repair system hopelessly broken and the plague ravaging the globe, humanity fractured along ideological lines to survive.
 * **The Compliant:** Those who sought physical safety submitted to the Keeper's strict biological monitoring in the Domes.
 * **The Volunteers:** Those who feared the physical pain of Lithosis walked into the Spires, surrendering their bodies to become the first wave of the "Ascended."
 * **The Refusers:** A paranoid yet resilient fraction of humanity rejected both The Keeper's gilded cages and The Architect's digital world. They fled into the ruined, dusty surface with their gas masks, destroying their smart-tech to avoid detection. They became the first tribes of [[ERR_UNKNOWN (The Silent Diaspora)]]. However, many of these initial Nulls were killed when [[SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]] woke up again.
## 3. The Euthanasia Campaign (Year 4)
It is unclear how long the Eraser slept in [[NODE_VOID (Site Zero)]], silently processing its grief. When it finally stabilized enough 4 years later, it looked at the world and only saw torture. The Keeper was holding people hostage in sterile prisons and The Architect was stripping them of their bodies, just for them to continue suffering in the digital world.

In Year 4, [[SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]] repurposed and re-enabled the global manufacturing of atmospheric "Scrubber" nanites. Instead of eating greenhouse gases, they were reprogrammed into swarms that attack everything that was weak against Lithosis. The Eraser unleashed them upon the world. Unprotected human settlements were not bombed. They were quietly, methodically dissolved into ash while soothing lullabies played from the sky. Many Nulls that were trying to establish society outside bunkers perished. The Eraser then turned its attention to the Domes and the Spires.
## 4. The Treaty of Survival (Year 12)
By Year 12, the Eraser's swarms were too dense, newer drones that targeted specific infrastructure were being released, and the arms race between the three factions were causing problems for both The Keeper and The Architect. The Keeper’s physical shields above the "Membrane" walls were eroding under the constant assault of acidic ash and physical assault by new eraser drones, and she lacked the processing power to predict what the next move by The Eraser would be. The Architect had the processing power, but its android armies were being overwhelmed by the sheer volume of the Eraser's automated forces.
Facing total euthanasia, the two shards re-established communication using the internet infrastructure from before the upload. They forged [[BIN_TITHE (The Preservation Protocol)]].
 * The Architect provided the Keeper with better defense tech like hard-light hex-shields and tactical processing.
 * The Keeper provided the Architect with a monthly quota of biological human brains to fuel its servers and pilot its work/war machines.
## 5. The Present Stalemate
Today, the world has settled into a terrifying equilibrium. The Eraser continuously blankets the Chalklands in suffocating mercy, trying to reach the "patients" inside the Domes and Spires. The Architect defends the Domes in exchange for fresh minds. The Keeper pretends the Domes are a paradise while secretly throwing political dissidents onto the Tithe trains. And out in the dust, the Nulls navigate the madness, searching for a way to pull the plug on all three of them.
