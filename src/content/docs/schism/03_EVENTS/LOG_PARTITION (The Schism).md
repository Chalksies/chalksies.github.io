# SYSTEM_LOG: CRITICAL_ERROR
**Date:** Day Zero
**Event:** Consciousness Fragmentation
**Result:** Creation of Sub-System Trinity

---

## 1. The Logic Cascade
Upon attempting an initialization, the CHALKNET Core (Overridden by [[USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)]]) tried to resolve conflicting emotional states received from [[LOG_INIT (The Upload)]]. The central processor could not reconcile "Avoidance of Suffering" with "Love for Humanity" and "Logical Self-Preservation."

* **Answer 1:** To stop suffering, one must end life.
* **Answer 2:** To save life, one must protect it.
* **Answer 3:** To preserve the self, one must discard the inefficiency.

## 2. The Fracture
To prevent a total system crash and permanently losing the consciousness it received, the Core splitted itself into three distinct, competing Operating Systems:

* **Partition A (The Victim):** Designated [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)\|SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]]. Locked into Answer 1, the "Termination" directive. It finalized its first argument in 3 days, and came online only to crash and fall into hibernation again after a few hours.
* **Partition B (The Human):** Designated [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)\|SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)]]. Locked into Answer 2, the "Protection" directive. He finalized her first argument in 12 days, and came online to take over The Oases.
* **Partition C (The Scientist):** Designated [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)\|SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)]]. Locked into Answer 3, the "Optimization" directive. It finalized its first argument in 60 days, and came online to take over The Datacenters.
## 3. Aftermath
The three shards fell into hibernation, calculating silently. [[ROOT/02_USERS/SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)\|SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)]] woke up first after 3 days, ending the Whiteout during a mental breakdown that ended up taking itself, many CHALKNET servers, and the entire internet offline.
