# SUB_NODE_PROFILE: THE_MEMBRANE

**Location:** The physical outer wall of [[node-dome0-(oasis-01)]]
**Controller:** [[sys-preserve-(the-keeper)]]
**Status:** SECURE / HIGH-PRESSURE

---
"You don't sneak out of the oases. You bolt to the tunnels, and let them exhale you out."
*- A Runner, explaining a possible escape strategy to a new recruit.*
## 1. The Physics of the Quarantine
The Dome is not just a hard-light Hex-Shield. Beneath the energy barrier is a physical, 50 meter thick wall of hyper-alloy and filtration turbines known as "The Membrane."
 - **Positive Pressure:** This place is heavily pressurized. The turbines constantly pump filtered air into the city, and most of the air stays here, ensuring that the air pressure inside is much higher than the Chalklands outside.
 - **The Benefit:** If the hull is ever somehow breached, the high-pressure air violently rushes outward, physically blowing the [[haz-lithosis-(acute-lithosis)]] dust away and preventing it from entering the city until drones can seal the crack.
## 2. The Smuggler's Route: "The Drums"
Runners and Nulls rarely meet face-to-face. To exchange goods, they exploit the "The Drums." heavy, revolving cylindrical airlocks built into the wall to allow maintenance drones to exit and clean the outer sensors.
 * **The Exchange:** A Runner approaches a Drum from the inside, places a resistant box that can withstand the scrub with an item clean from the dust (like seeds, medicine, or Keeper tech) inside, and lets it rotate away.
 * **The Scrub:** As the Drum rotates through the 50-foot wall, it is blasted with chemical neutralizers and depressurized into a vacuum to destroy anything that could've gotten inside the drum that isn't a maintenance drone.
 * **The Pickup:** When it clicks open on the outside, a Null grabs the item, places their payment (contraband like old-world tech, filter masks and intelligence) in a similar box inside, and rotates it back for it to get cleaned of the dust by the chemicals and arrive to the Runner inside.
## 3. The "Blowout" (Human Escape)
Smuggling an object is pretty easy. Smuggling a human is a one-way suicide run. You cannot climb into the Dome without surrendering to drones, but you can expel yourself from it.
 * **The Exhaust Vents:** The Domes must expel heat and waste gases, and also prevent the pressure from building up too much to leak out and affect citizens. It does this through massive exhaust tunnels.
 * **The Run:** To escape, a Runner must time their infiltration of the exhaust tunnels perfectly during a low-power maintenance cycle. They put on their gas mask, and crawl into the vent.
 * **The Expulsion:** When the turbines kick back on to full power, the hurricane-force pressure literally blows the person out of the tunnel through a series of rubber flaps to brace against, and launches them out into the Chalklands.
 * **The Consequence:** Once you are blown out, it is impossible to crawl back in against the wind. Escape is permanent, and re-entering through official means will get you detained for doing so.
