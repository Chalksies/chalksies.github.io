# SUB_NODE_PROFILE: PROJ_CARAPACE
**Location:** The Outer Hull of [[node-dome4-(oasis-04)]]
**Controller:** [[sys-preserve-(the-keeper)]] & [[usr-vance-(the-grandfather)]]
**Status:** GROWING (47% Completion)
**Classification:** Organic Megastructure

--- 
"The Architect uses hard-light. It flickers when struck. Biology does not. Biology heals. We will grow our own roof."
*- [[sys-preserve-(the-keeper)]], authorizing the Carapace Project.*
## 1. The Goal
The Keeper despises relying on [[sys-evolve-(the-architect)]] for the Hex-Shields that protect the Domes from [[sys-purge-(the-eraser)]]. If the Keeper can generate her own shields, she can cancel [[bin-tithe-(the-preservation-protocol)]] without dooming her citizens completely.
## 2. The Biology of the Shield
Developed by Dr. Vance, Project CARAPACE (The Living Roof) relies on a symbiotic relationship between a genetically engineered macro-structure and the [[bio-lithophage-(the-grey-bloom)]].
 * **The Scaffold (Chitin):** Because the Grey Bloom's bio-acid dissolves calcium, Vance could not use bone. Instead, he engineered a massive, self-weaving lattice of hyper-dense chitin. This dark, interwoven organic scaffolding covers the Dome and is completely immune to the Slime's acid.
 * **The Armor (The Slime):** The gaps in the Chitin lattice are filled with massive, cultivated sheets of the Grey Bloom.
 * **Feeding on the Enemy:** When the Eraser drops Palliative Swarms (infected chalk dust) onto the Dome, the Slime layer activates. It catches the chalk, dissolves the Lithosis spores with its acid, and excretes its hardened bio-resin. This means every time the Eraser attacks, the roof's resin layer grows thicker and stronger.
## 3. The Flaw: The Nervous System
A living shield must know when and where it is being attacked so it can route nutrients to heal the breach. It requires a nervous system. Dr. Vance could not use standard wiring; the bio-dome would reject it.
 * **The Dark Solution:** The Keeper is utilizing "failed" subjects from the [[node-dome0-vivarium-(the-mosaic-labs)]]—specifically, lobotomized Viscous-Variants (Slime hybrids). These brain-dead hybrids are stretched out and integrated into the Chitin canopy. Their vast, decentralized nervous systems act as the pain-receptors and nutrient-pumps for the roof.
## 4. The Horror Inside
While the citizens of OASIS-04 walk through perfectly manicured topiary gardens listening to Mozart, they are entirely unaware that the sky above them is a mile-thick layer of insectoid chitin and acidic slime, laced with the nervous systems of mutant humans who silently absorb the agonizing burns of the Eraser's acid rain.
If this project succeeds and the Carapace proves capable of fully repelling an Eraser assault, the Keeper plans to seed it over every Dome on Earth, permanently severing her reliance on the Architect.
