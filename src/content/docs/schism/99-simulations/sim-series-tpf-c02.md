# SIMULATION_LOG: SERIES_THE_PALE_FREQUENCY_C02

**Title:** The Distrust 
**Type:** Log Reconstruction 
**Location:** A [[prot-null-(the-zero-directive)]] bunker in [[zone-unmapped-(the-chalklands)]], former Malaysian territory. 
**Status:** ARCHIVED

## 1. Context Parameters

**Time:** 07:30 (Early Morning). **Setting:** A repurposed underground bunker in the outskirts of what used to be Johor Bahru, owned by a group of Nulls.

## 2. Playback Sequence

Jax opens the door, revealing a huge, industrial yet warm area with tens of people chatting, playing games, and engaging in various other activities.

**Jax:** "I didn't expect it to be so.. spacious."

The Null gently pushes him, forcing Jax to walk downstairs while drawing attention. He walks down, trying to hide his nerves. He makes his way towards the other side of the giant room, ignoring the stares.

The Null sits down on an empty couch and pats the space next to him. Jax also sits down.

**Null:** "So... a Runner that escaped the dome. Why? Did the nannies give you expired paste?"

**Jax:** "No, I-- I..."

He sighs, hesitating to tell about the casette, but he doesn't have any other choice. He can't keep this kind of information to himself.

**Jax:** "...Jabat gave me a casette and asked me for a map. I wasn't planning on running away, but the casette had that ghost lady in it and whatever's watching the people in the dome heard it. I had to escape in like, ten seconds."

**Null:** "Right.. I did hear about a secret casette in his possession. So you have it now..."

He extends a hand.

**Null:** "You can call me Aaron."

Jax shakes his hand firmly, still secretly nervous.

**Jax:** "Alright, Aaron... I'm Jax."

A few interested Nulls begin gathering near the two, curious about the new person.

**Jax:** "So, uh... I.. the casette says that there is a key to a.. backdoor?
Somewhere in Sector 44--"

**Aaron:** "Backdoor to what?"

**Jax:** "CHALKNET servers I assume? It talked about the upload or something, I reallt don't know, ask Jabat... Also, I'm very thirsty and hungry. Been walking for hours..."
