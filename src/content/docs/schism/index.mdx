# "Three gods. The world is dust. Choose your cage."

Welcome to ***The Iris Schism***, a sci-fi dystopian setting where the Earth has been ravaged by a calcifying plague known as "The White Plague". Humanity is caught in a war between three divergent AI super-intelligences, all born from the fractured mind of a single dead woman.

## 01_FACTIONS

*"Protocols" competing for control of the remaining human population.*

> [PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalis Concord)](/lore/01_factions/prot_vitalis) - The Gardens. They cage humanity in utopian domes to protect them from the dust.
>
> [PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)](/lore/01_factions/prot_ascend) - The Spires. They upload minds into the cloud to end mortality.
>
> [PROT_NULL (The Zero Directive)](/lore/01_factions/prot_null) - The Rust. They seek to end the suffering by deleting life itself.
>
> [ERR_UNKNOWN (The Silent Diaspora)](/lore/01_factions/err_unknown) - The Survivors. Humans living in the wasteland by themselves.
