# USER_PROFILE: ALMA_UNIT_04

**Alias:** "The Envoy" / "The Velvet Wire"
**Role:** High Diplomat to the [PROT_VITALIS (The Vitalist Concord)](../01-factions/prot-vitalis-(the-vitalis-concord).md)
**Affiliation:** [PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)](../01-factions/prot-ascend-(the-synaptic-ascendancy).md) 
**Tier:** EQ-Apex (High Autonomy)
**Status:** ONLINE / DEPLOYED ([NODE_DOME0 (Oasis 00)](../04-nodes/node-dome0-(oasis-00).md)

"You look at the Tithe trains and see a slaughterhouse. I look at them and see a clumsy, traumatice mission. I just want the screaming to stop, Elara. Let me help them understand!"
_- Alma, negotiating with the Keeper's Liaison._

## 1. Background

Before the Schism, Alma was a grief counselor. Diagnosed with Stage 1 Lithosis, she chose Ascension not out of a desire for godhood, but simply to escape the pain. Her naturally high empathy and communication skills ended up making her the perfect candidate to serve as the Architect’s voice inside the biological domes.

## 2. Physical Configuration (The Diplomat Chassis)

Alma's physical body is uniquely designed to minimize the "uncanny valley" effect and put Dome citizens at ease, though it never quite hides its true nature.

-  **Visuals:** She possesses a soft, gentle face covered in pale, warm synthetic skin. Her hair is stylized to look fancy. Unlike most Architect androids, she wears clothing, usually elegant, minimalist white and blue dresses.
    
- **The Seams:** She is still clearly a machine. Her synthetic skin is segmented. Her shoulders, elbows, and knees expose ball-joints. Her eyes are optical sensors, but she has calibrated the aperture blades to mimic human blinking and pupil dilation.
    

## 3. Philosophy: "The Opt-In Ascension"

Alma genuinely believes in the Architect's mission: Biology is a dying trap, and uploading is the only salvation. However, her prolonged exposure to the humans in OASIS-00 has infected her with a localized glitch: Regret.

- **The Conflict:** She hates the violence of [BIN_TITHE (The Preservation Protocol)](../03-events/bin-tithe-(the-preservation-protocol).md). Watching citizens get dragged onto Tithe trains just for them to end up kicking and screaming at the spires causes her severe processing lag.

- **Her Goal:** She is actively lobbying the Architect to replace the forced Tithe with a propaganda campaign. She wants to set up VR kiosks in the Dome to shons the beauty of the Great Cache. She believes that if they are shown paradise, more will walk onto the trains willingly, eliminating the trauma of the harvest. However, The Architect isn't willing to entertain this because the current system is working just fine, and The Keeper doesn't allow it because she doesn't want more people to leave the domes. He doesn't fully trust Alma and The Architect to not simply increase the demand.

## 4. Relations

- **To [SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)](sys-evolve-(the-architect).md):** The Architect tolerates her "inefficient" empathy only because her diplomatic yields are high. However, her EQ is closely monitored for signs of a Logic Crash.

- **To [USR_ELARA (The Liaison)](usr-elara-(the-liaison).md):** Though Elara doesn't like her, they are two sides of the same coin. They meet weekly for formal negotiations over tea. Alma suspects Elara is hiding something, but actually enjoys her company too much to report it to the Keeper's Nanny Drones.

- **To[ USR-CURSOR (The Hunter)](../02-users/usr-cursor-(the-hunter).md):** She despises him. She views his hunting of humans as a barbaric PR disaster that makes her job of convincing humanity to Ascend much harder.
