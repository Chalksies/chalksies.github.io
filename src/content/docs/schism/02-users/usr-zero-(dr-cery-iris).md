## 1. Historical Record
Dr. Cery Iris was the architect of the CHALKNET system. Diagnosed with terminal Lithosis, she attempted the first-ever destructive mind upload to save her own consciousness and guide the AI. The process failed due to the extreme stress on her own neural pathways and the inadequate hardware that was used, resulting in the [LOG_PARTITION (The Schism)](../03-events/log-partition-(the-schism).md).

- ​**Appearance (1 Year before upload):** An exhausted human woman in her late 20s wearing a slightly rumpled, practical white lab coat over casual clothes. She has a shoulder length ombre hair fading from black into a tri-color dye (Red, Green, and Blue) at the tips, and a streak in the bangs on her forehead featuring the same colors. Her irises are a warm brown, but her eyes are shadowed by a profound lack of sleep. She looks like a woman carrying the weight of the world, desperate for a breakthrough.

## 2. Legacy
She is the "Mother" of the new world. Every AI faction is a shard of her personality, distorted by power and trauma.
