# USER_PROFILE: SUBJECT_LUMI

**System ID:** VIVARIUM_SUB_12-L (Escaped)
**Alias:** "The Spirit of the Campus" / "The Puddle"
**Species:** Viscous-Variant Hybrid (Slime-Base)
**Location:** [ NODE_ISOLATION (Sector 44](../04-nodes/node-isolation-(sector-44))
**Status:** ACTIVE / INTEGRATED

## 1. Background

Lumi was an early test subject of [NODE_DOME0_VIVARIUM_HAB12 (The Gel City)](../04-nodes/node-dome0-vivarium-hab12-(the-gel-city).md).

- **The Escape:** During a waste-disposal flush, she liquefied herself completely and was washed out into the ocean. She drifted for years until she found the coastline of the Korean Peninsula.   
- **The Sanctuary:** She discovered the ruins of a pre-war University (Sector 44). The geothermal heating system was still active. She crawled inside the pipes to survive the freezing winter and never left.
    

## 2. Physiology: "The Building is Alive"

Lumi has fully integrated into the campus infrastructure.

- **Dispersed Form:** She rarely maintains a humanoid shape. She exists as hundreds of gallons of bioluminescent gel filling the university's heating ducts, plumbing, and steam tunnels.
- **Avatar:** When she needs to communicate, she oozes out of a vent or a drain and forms a translucent, glowing feminine humanoid.
- **Weakness:** Cold. If the campus geothermal generator fails, she will need to find another warm place to live in. She keeps it functioning together with [USR_IRIS_BETA (The Backup)](usr-iris-beta-(the-backup).md)
    

## 3. Personality

- **The Big Sister:** She found the [USR_IRIS_BETA (The Backup)](usr-iris-beta-(the-backup).md) server in the basement. She doesn't fully understand what it is, but she knows it is a child that is pure and lonely. She protects the server room with ferocious loyalty, and keeps the Beta-AI company.
- **Communication:** She initially scavenged a voice module from an android, but doesn't use it. She speaks through campus speakers with a synthetic voice created by [USR_IRIS_BETA (The Backup)](usr-iris-beta-(the-backup).md)
    

## 4. Combat Capabilities

Intruders (Scavengers or Drones) often underestimate her.

- **The Drowning:** She can fill a room with herself in seconds, drowning enemies in viscous fluid.    
- **The Burn:** She can manipulate her acidity to melt drone shields.
- **The Brute-force:** If not inside campus, she uses metal parts and manipulates her consistency to turn her arms into maces.
