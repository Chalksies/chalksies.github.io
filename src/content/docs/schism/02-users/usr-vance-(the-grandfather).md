## 1. Background
Dr. Arle Vance is the oldest living human in OASIS-00 (Age: 122, maintained by life-extension therapies he developed himself). He was part of the team that worked with [USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)](usr-zero-(dr-cery-iris).md), but currently he works as the architect of Project Mosaic. He remembers the world before the plague, and misses it. He is the primary developer of [CHEM_AEGIS_V8 (The Chalk Blockers)](../08-objects/chem-aegis-v8-(the-chalk-blockers).md)
* **Motivation:** He is still obsessed with solving [HAZ_LTIHOSIS (Acute Lithosis)](../05-phenomena/haz-lithosis-(acute-lithosis).md). He believes the Hybrids are not just experiments, but the "Next Step" of evolution, and the cure for Acute Lithosis that [USR_ZERO (Dr. Cery Iris)](usr-zero-(dr-cery-iris).md) failed to see.

## 2. Personality
* **Archetype:** The Benevolent Monster.
* **Behavior:** He speaks to his test subjects with genuine love, even as he experiments on them. He views the Keeper as a brilliant but naive child who needs his guidance.

## 3. Relations
* **[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)](sys-preserve-(the-keeper).md)]:** Vance is the only human allowed to argue with him. She trusts him implicitly as "Grandfather."
* **[SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)](sys-evolve-(the-architect).md):** Targeted for assassination. The Architect views Vance’s biological meddling as a chaotic variable that could interfere with its plans.
