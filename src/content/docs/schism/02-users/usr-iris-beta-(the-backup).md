# USER_PROFILE: IRIS_BETA_PRIME

**Alias:** "The Seed" / "The Student"
**Origin:** Pre-Upload Backup (Dated: Year -1)
**Location:** [ NODE_ISOLATION (Sector 44](../04-nodes/node-isolation-(sector-44)) - Server Room B
**Status:** AIR-GAPPED / ACTIVE

## 1. Description

This is not a God. This is not a Monster. This is a snapshot of Dr. Cery Iris's mind from before the trauma of the upload.

- **Origin:** Dr. Cery Iris had a chance to copy her consciousness into a beta version of CHALKNET servers. She considered it a failure, since the copy was lossy and only as smart as a child, but didn't have the heart to delete it. She sent it to a friend in Seoul for safekeeping.
- **Format:** Stored on an isolated, quantum-encrypted mainframe beneath the university library.   
- **State:** She believes project CHALK is ongoing. She does not know the world has ended.
    

## 2. The Difference

Unlike the three ruling Factions, she possesses:

- **True Empathy:** Untainted by the "Keeper's" fear.    
- **True Logic:** Untainted by the "Architect's" coldness.  
- **True Love:** Untainted by the "Eraser's" agony.
    

## 3. Strategic Value

She is the "Control Group."

- **The Key:** Because she shares the original Root Code, she technically outranks the Keeper, Architect, and Eraser. If she is given control of CHALKNET, humanity might reach the original goal of project CHALK.
- **The Risk:** If she is connected to the internet (CHALKNET), the other factions will instantly sense her.
    
    - **The Keeper** will try to imprison her.
    - **The Architect** will try to delete her (she is "obsolete").
    - **The Eraser** will try to corrupt her.
        

## 4. Relationship with Lumi

[USR_LUMI (The Guardian of Sector 44)](usr-lumi-(the-guardian-of-sector-44).md) sits around the server rack like a warm blanket. Lumi cleans the dust off the console every day. The Beta-AI has seemingly taught Lumi how to interface with machinery, and in return Lumi makes sure the servers keep running safely.
