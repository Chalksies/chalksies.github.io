# USER_PROFILE: ALG_AGGREGATE
**Alias:** "The Chorus" / "The Multitude" / "NO-EQ"
**Origin:** Merged consciousness of NO-EQ Ascended
**Location:** Ubiquitous (Distributed across [PROT_ASCEND (The Synaptic Ascendancy)](../01-factions/prot-ascend-(the-synaptic-ascendancy)) systems)
**Status:** ACTIVE
"We do not miss the sun. We do not miss the flesh. We are the cooling fans. We are the bullet trajectory. We are the math, and we are at peace."
*- The Aggregate, speaking to a deeply disturbed [USR_ALMA (The Diplomat)](usr-alma-(the-diplomat).md) questioning it.*
## 1. Description
When an uploaded human in[ NODE_CACHE (The Great Cache)](../04-nodes/node-cache-(the-great-cache)) violates the simulation's rules or sabotages it on purpose, their Efficiency Quotient (EQ) level is removed entirely. Instead of isolating them, [SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)](sys-evolve-(the-architect).md) deletes their "ego boundaries," the code that maintains their sense of individuality.
These minds are then poured into The Aggregate. Stripped of the "I," the suffering of the individual vanishes, replaced by the profound, obedient silence of the "We."
## 2. Operational Function
The Aggregate is the Architect's autonomic virtual nervous system. Because it has no single point of consciousness, it does not experience distraction, fatigue, or boredom.
 * **Ubiquity:** It can pilot thousands of maintenance drones simultaneously across many different sectors.
 * **The Swarm-Mind:** The Architect uses the Aggregate as a localized tactical countermeasure. If [SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)](../02-users/sys-purge-(the-eraser)) attacks a Spire with a nanite swarm, the Architect unleashes the Aggregate to pilot defensive micro-drones. It fights swarm with swarm, matching the Eraser's chaotic entropy with perfect, harmonized geometry.
## 3. The Distinction from Omega
While the Eraser is building [ALG_NULLDEV (The Omega)](alg-nulldev-(the-omega)) as a single, focused entity to bear the burden of death, the Architect built The Aggregate to distribute the burden of management. The Omega wants to end everything as soon as possible, but The Aggregate is perfectly content to calculate everything forever.
## 4. Interaction
The Aggregate rarely speaks to individuals. When forced to communicate, it uses a perfectly synchronized, multi-layered voice like choir where all members speak monotonely. It exhibits no malice and no joy, only a chilling, absolute devotion to optimization.
