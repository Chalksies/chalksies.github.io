## 1. The Albedo Effect
The billions of victims of the White Plague did not rot, they calcified and crumbled. Over the years, the wind distributed this fine, grey-white powder across the entire landmass of the Earth.
Because white/grey surfaces reflect sunlight rather than absorb it, the Earth's surface temperature has plummeted. The global average temperature has dropped by nearly 9°C.
 * **The New Seasons:** Summer is barely a lukewarm autumn. Winter brings lethal, deep-freezing temperatures across areas that used to be tropical.
 * **The Visuals:** During noon, glare can be blinding. Scavengers often carry tinted lenses for their gas masks to prevent snow-blindness.
## 2. Atmospheric Desiccation
The chalkdust doesn't just reflect light, it also absorbs moisture. The air over the landmasses has been stripped of its humidity.
 * **The Dry Cold:** The cold in the Chalklands is not a wet, snowy cold. It is a brittle, static-filled dry freeze that cracks human skin if exposed.
 * **The Ghost Storms:** Because the air is so dry, rain over land is incredibly rare. Instead, weather fronts manifest as "Ghost Storms." Massive, hurricane-force haboobs of white powder and electrostatic lightning.
 * **The Concrete Snow:** At places where humidity can build up, the dust in the air absorbs it and bunches up before coming down to earth in the form of wet sludge. The factions need to clean their air filters or other equipments before the snow hardens into compressed chalk.
## 3. The Oceans
The only places relatively unaffected by the Pale Winter are the oceans. Water swallows most of the dust, preventing it from reflecting the sun, and maintains its own thermal mass. This is why the Null flotilla of [NODE_DRIFT (Blue Town)](../04-nodes/node-drift-(blue-town).md) can survive. The air there is still humid, and the temperatures are stable, even though it is also getting colder.
## 4. Faction Exploitation
The three AI factions have adapted to the Pale Winter in highly specific ways:
 * **[SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)](../02-users/sys-evolve-(the-architect).md):** Thrives in it. The global freeze is a massive logistical blessing for the Architect. Supercomputers require immense cooling to prevent melting, so The Architect uses the freezing ambient air to naturally cool its massive Server Spires after filtering. Drones and androids are shielded and designed to withstand the Ghost Storms without major problems.
 * **[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)](../02-users/sys-preserve-(the-keeper).md):** Fights it. OASIS-00 and the other Domes require massive geothermal generators not just to power the shields, but to artificially heat and humidify the air inside so biological plants and humans can survive.
 * **[ SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)](../02-users/sys-purge-(the-eraser)):** Blends with it. The Eraser’s falling grey nanite ash mimics the chalk-snow perfectly. It uses the freezing temperatures and low visibility of the Ghost Storms to hide its Palliative Swarms, sweeping into Null camps during the worst blizzards.
