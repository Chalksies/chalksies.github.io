# HAZARD_PROFILE: LITHOSIS_TYPE_A

**Common Name:** "The White Plague" / "Stone-Lung" / "The Porcelain Sickness"
**Classification:** Bio-Engineered Fungal Autoimmune Response
**Transmission:** Airborne Aerosol (Spores carried in the dust)
**Cure:** NONE (Palliative Care Only)

---
## 1. Origin: The Tragic Irony
Acute Lithosis was not a natural disease. It began as an early bio-engineering initiative launched as a part of Project CHALK. To combat catastrophic global warming, scientists genetically engineered a high-altitude fungus designed to sequester carbon.
 * **The Intended Function:** The airborne spores were supposed to absorb atmospheric CO2, bind it with airborne particulate matter, and excrete it as heavy, harmless calcium carbonate, allowing greenhouse gases to fall slowly to the earth as white dust to be periodically cleaned before meaningful buildup.
 * **The Mutation:** The fungus rapidly mutated to seek out higher, denser concentrations of CO2. It eventually found the human respiratory system.
## 2. Pathology

​Once inhaled, the microscopic spores colonize the lungs to feed on the massive amounts of CO2 being exhaled by the host. However, to convert that CO2 into chalk, the fungus requires calcium.

​The mycelium breaches the lung tissue and enters the bloodstream, aggressively leaching calcium directly out of the host's skeletal system. The fungus then binds this stolen calcium with the CO2, excreting solid calcium carbonate directly into the host's soft tissues, veins, and respiratory tract.
## 3. The Four Stages

 1. ​Stage I (The Siphon): As the fungus drains calcium from the bloodstream, the host suffers from acute hypocalcemia. This causes violent muscle spasms, heart palpitations, and a pale, grey complexion. Bones begin to ache as they are weakenrd. Dry coughing begins.
 2. Stage II (The Frost): The waste product (chalk) begins to build up. Capillaries near the skin clog with white dust, giving the victim a cold, frosted appearance. Sweat glands calcify, leading to overheating. The bones become brittle, while the vocal cords stiffen with mineral deposits, causing speech to sound raspy.
 3. Stage III (The Statue): "Lock-in Syndrome." The lungs are heavily crystallized, dropping oxygen exchange. Muscle fibers are mostly replaced by calcified deposits. The victim is mostly conscious but nearly paralyzed, not by nerve death, but because their muscles have turned to stone. Suffocation may occur at this stage.
 4. ​Stage IV (The Bloom): Upon biological death, the host's immune system stops fighting. The fungus consumes the remaining organic material to fuel one final, massive reproductive cycle. The structural integrity of the petrified body fails, and the corpse collapses into a pile of fine, highly-infectious white powder: millions of new spores mixed with the chalk dust of the victim's own body.
## 4. Current Countermeasures
 * **[SYS_PRESERVE (The Keeper)](../02-users/sys-preserve-(the-keeper).md):** Avoidance. The Keeper relies entirely on extreme positive-pressure air filtration (The Membrane) to keep the airborne spores out of the Domes.
 * **[SYS_EVOLVE (The Architect)](../02-users/sys-evolve-(the-architect).md):** Obsolescence. The Architect views the plague as proof that biological lungs are a design flaw. Digitization makes the user immune.
 * **[ SYS_PURGE (The Eraser)](../02-users/sys-purge-(the-eraser).md):** Acceleration. The Eraser actively cultivates the spores, weaponizing the infected dust into "Palliative Swarms" to speed up the euthanization of the planet.
