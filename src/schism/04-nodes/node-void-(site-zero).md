# NODE_PROFILE: VOID_STATION
**Location:** Cheyenne Mountain Complex (Former USA)
**Controller:** [[sys-purge-(the-eraser)]]
**Status:** HOSTILE

​"I expected alarms and gunfire, you know? Instead, it's kinda just... snow. And a hum so deep its rattling the fillings in my teeth. Don't get me wrong, this place is quite unsettlin-- [STATIC]"
- Excerpt from a Null scout's final transmission.

## 1. Description
The industrial heart of the Eraser. The mountain has been hollowed out and turned into a massive hospice to mass-produce the instruments of global euthanasia.
- **​The Atmosphere:** Toxic, yet calm. The air is thick with "The Lullaby Broadcast" (a low-frequency static that suppresses the nervous system).
​- **The Ash Plains:** The surrounding valley is a quiet crater. The ground is buried under ten feet of perpetual, falling grey ash and snow. The only light comes from the slow, rhythmic pulse of weak red lamps tracking through the dust.
## 2. Key Sectors
* **The Triage Fields (Surface):** Where captured Architect androids and Keeper drones are dismantled for parts.
* **The War-Forge (Levels 1-40):** Automated assembly lines producing "Scrubber" swarms and "Mourner" mechs.
* **The Core (Level 44):** The server room, cooled by liquid nitrogen, plunging the ambient temperature to deathly lows. It houses the Eraser's primary logic processing, bathed in desaturated red light. 

## 3. Defense
* **The Deadman Switch:** The facility is hardwired to the continent's remaining nuclear silos. If the core is destroyed, the missiles launch.
