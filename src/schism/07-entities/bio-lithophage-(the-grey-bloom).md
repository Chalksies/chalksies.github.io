# ENTITY_PROFILE: LITHOPHAGE_COLONY
**Common Name:** "The Grey Bloom" / "Chalk-Eaters" / "The Slime"
**Classification:** Mutated Extremophile Slime-Mold
**Origin:** Natural Evolution in [[zone-unmapped-(the-chalklands)]]
**Hazard Level:** MODERATE (Highly corrosive, but slow-moving)
"We finally found something that eats the plague. The only problem is that it eats the patient, too."
*- [[usr-vance-(the-grandfather)]], abandoning early Slime-Cure trials.*
## 1. Biology & Evolution
While humanity died or hid, a new organism evolved in the wasteland to exploit the most abundant resource on Earth: the infected chalk dust.
 * **The Mechanism:** Rhe slime secretes an incredibly potent, localized bio-acid. This acid violently dissolves both the solid calcium carbonate and the Lithosis spores hidden inside it. The slime metabolizes the dead fungus for energy, binds the calcium into its own viscous cellular structure, and excretes an inert, hardened bio-resin.
 * **Immunity:** It is the only biological entity completely immune to [[haz-lithosis-(acute-lithosis)]], because it views the plague as a food source.
## 2. The Failed Cure
During the early days of the Schism, Dr. Vance attempted to weaponize the Grey Bloom into a cure. The experiments were failures.

To break the chemical bonds of the Lithosis chalk, the Slime's bio-acid is intensely corrosive. If a human ingests the Slime, it successfully hunts and kills the Lithosis fungus in their lungs, but it also rapidly liquefies the host's internal organs and soft tissue.
## 3. Behavior in the Wild
 * **The Carpet:** In ruins with high body counts (like old hospitals or quarantine zones), the Bloom covers the floor like a living, translucent grey carpet, slowly dissolving the calcified statues of the dead.
 * **Movement:** Slow but relentless. It operates on a "Hive-Cell" instinct, inching toward calcium heat-signatures.
 * **Defense:** If threatened, it can manipulate its consistency, hardening its outer layer into the tough bio-resin it normally excretes.
## 4. Faction Exploitation
 * **[[sys-purge-(the-eraser)]]:** Hates it. The Eraser views the slime as a grotesque mockery of life. Furthermore, the slime's acid gums up the gears of its drones and actively eats the Palliative Swarms.
 * **[[sys-evolve-(the-architect)]]:** Fascinated. The Architect views the slime as rare "Biological Efficiency."
 * **[[sys-preserve-(the-keeper)]]:** Weaponizes it. Since Dr. Vance couldn't cure humans with the slime, he began splicing human sentience *into* the slime (See: [[node-dome0-vivarium-hab12-(the-gel-city)]]). Furthermore, he is currently cultivating massive sheets of it to grow the Living Roof of [[node-dome4-(oasis-04)]]
